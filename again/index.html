Everything you need to turn “I'll go tomorrow” into a streak you don't want to break — and none of the bloat you'd never use.

Log a set in seconds

Weight, reps, done. The whole app is built to get you logged and back to your rest timer with the fewest possible taps.

A streak that pulls you in

Every workout feeds the flame. Set a weekly goal, get a gentle nudge, and let the streak do the motivating.

Personal records, celebrated

Again spots your PRs automatically — heaviest set, best estimated max — and marks the moment so progress is impossible to miss.

Progress you can see

Volume, sets, and trends per exercise over time — a clear read on whether you're actually getting stronger.

Every exercise, your way

A ready-made library of lifts grouped by muscle, plus your own custom exercises for whatever your training needs.

Yours, on your device

No account, no feed, no friends to beat. Choose kg or lb, export your data anytime, and train in peace.
